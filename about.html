About Pardesi Consultancy

Pardesi Consultancy is focused on helping Indian applicants plan their journey to Canada with clarity and confidence. Instead of confusion and mixed information, our aim is to provide straightforward guidance that respects both your time and your goals.

Our Approach

We combine real-world experience with practical understanding of Canadian pathways — including education, PR, work permits and long-term settlement planning.

  • Honest conversations about what is realistic
  • Clear explanations in English, Hindi & Punjabi
  • Step-by-step direction instead of generic advice

Who We Serve

We primarily support applicants based in India and Indian residents already in Canada who want to better understand their study, PR or work options.

  • Students considering Canada for higher education
  • Skilled workers exploring PR or PNP options
  • Couples and families planning long-term settlement
