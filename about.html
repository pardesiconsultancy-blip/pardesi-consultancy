About Pardesi Consultancy

Pardesi Consultancy was founded to help students, professionals, and families from India achieve their Canadian education and long-term settlement dreams — with honesty, clarity, and proper guidance.

Pooja Tiwari

Founder & Owner

Pooja is deeply committed to helping students and families make well-planned, confident decisions about their future in Canada. Her strengths lie in student counselling, SOP structuring, college/program selection, and preparing strong supporting documents that match Canadian expectations.

With a compassionate and detail-oriented approach, she ensures clients understand every step clearly and feel supported throughout their journey.

Sahil Vashisht

Founder & Owner

Sahil brings a strong understanding of immigration pathways, profile evaluation, and planning strategies for PR, work permits, and long-term settlement options in Canada.

He focuses on providing clear, practical advice based on eligibility, ensuring clients follow the right pathway instead of wasting time or money. His guidance helps applicants avoid common mistakes that lead to refusals or delays.
