About Pardesi Consultancy

Pardesi Consultancy was created with one purpose — to help students, skilled workers, and families from India achieve their Canadian education, career, and settlement goals with honesty, clarity, and personalized guidance.

Sahil Vashisht

Co-Founder & CEO

With hands-on experience supporting newcomers, guiding international students, and understanding the real challenges Indians face when planning a move to Canada, Sahil specializes in providing the right direction from day one.

His focus is on strategic planning, identifying eligibility pathways, and helping clients avoid the common mistakes that lead to delays or refusals.

Pooja Tiwari

Co-Founder & CEO

Pooja brings a compassionate and detail-oriented approach to student guidance, documentation support, and visa presentation. Being deeply familiar with the expectations of Canadian institutions and visa officers, she ensures every file is well-prepared and professionally structured.

Her strengths include student counselling, SOP assistance, and helping families prepare for long-term settlement success.
