About Us

We are Pooja Tiwari and Sahil Vashisht, the founders of Pardesi Consultancy — a husband-and-wife team dedicated to guiding students and families through their Canadian journey.

We both came to Canada as international students, facing the same challenges, confusion, fear, and excitement that every newcomer goes through. We handled college admissions, program changes, visas, study extensions, work permits, and later, permanent residency. These experiences shaped our understanding deeply — not from theory, but from real life.

Sahil eventually achieved his Canadian Permanent Residency, and together we decided to use our life lessons, mistakes, and knowledge to guide others. Today, we proudly help students, couples, and families confidently plan their pathways to Canada with clarity and honesty.

We treat every case the way we treated our own journey — with care, discipline, transparency, and a “family-first” mindset.
